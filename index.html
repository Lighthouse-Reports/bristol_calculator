Inside Bristol’s Think Family Database

The Think Family Database (TFD) was created in 2015 by Insight Bristol, a partnership data analytics team between Avon and Somerset Police and Bristol City Council.

The Council claims that the TFD’s goal is intended to make prevention and intervention for vulnerable families more targeted and effective. Critics worry that it surveils and profiles already marginalized families.

To build this system, the TFD collects vast amounts of data about parents and children -- as well as their relatives -- in Bristol. It pulls together information from roughly 35 distinct datasets.
